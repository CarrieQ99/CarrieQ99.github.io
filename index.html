"When you buy a kitten, and with your precious dollars part, You only pay with money... We pay with a piece of our heart..." Close your eyes and imagine the archetypal cat. A cobby, four-square cat ..…... a people cat ..…... a "companion" cat ....... a lap-cat ....... and occasionally "I’ll sit on your lap when I feel like it, thank you!!” cat ....... a purring machine on short sturdy legs ....... a "teddy-bear” cat ....... in fact, the feline friend for life.

Welcome to Feliland - Feline's Land... We are family-house based cattery. Our cattery is registered with The Cat Fanciers' Association (CFA) and The International Cat Association (TICA). We work with the best champion bloodlines from Europe and breed plush British Shorthair Kittens in a rare Black Silver Shaded, Black Golden Shaded and Chinchilla colors. We give genetic and health guarantee. Our kittens have blue, aqua and green eyes. We are two sisters, Anna and Olga, we are originally from Russia and moved to US in 1998. Olga is the one who started Feliland … She brought her first female Lissy from Austria in 2009 and just couldn't help but fell in love with her aqua eyes and her sweet and aristocratic personality. Anna has joined Olga in 2011. The biggest helpers are Tatyana – Olga's daughter and Diana and Sarah – Anna's daughters.

Also we have a Gerbil named Garfield, Marty the Chinchilla and Cocker Spaniel named Max. . Our kittens are well socialized, healthy and affectionate. Never caged, they are raised underfoot and on the lap. Our focus is on health, personality and finding just the right lovely home for our babies. If you are looking for a beautiful silver white or golden kitten with adorable eyes and an endearing personality, Please contact us.
